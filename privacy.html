Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Fixtock ("we", "our", or "us") provides business operations and multi-location management tools. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

To provide our core app functionality, we collect the following information which is linked directly to your user identity:

- Contact Information: First Name, (optional Last Name), Email Address, and Phone Number.

- Business & Location Data: Physical worksite and warehouse addresses.

These identifiers are merely for users feedback when using our App. Hence, pseudonym or alias are permitted as well.

How We Use Information

We use your data solely for App Functionality, including authenticating your login, tracking inventory across your locations, and providing customer support. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or tracking purposes.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect business and user information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services (such as Firebase for cloud storage and authentication). These providers only access your data to perform tasks on our behalf and are contractually obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Data Retention

We retain your personal and business data only for as long as your account is active to service your operations. Authroized users can also delete entire business data, including employee's infomration, by long-pressing the company profile and following the prompt.

Account Deletion Rights:You have the right to delete your account at any time (by long-pressing your company profile). Deletion occurs immediately upon requesting account deletion within the app. You may also contact us at support@fixtock.com, we will permanently delete your personal profile identifiers and associated data from our active databases in compliance with App/Play Store guidelines.
